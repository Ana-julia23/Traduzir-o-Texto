Inglês

Languages around the world play a crucial role globally, but have you ever thought about the immense number of languages that exist around the world? With more than 7,000 languages spoken today, they are not just tools for communication; they are fundamental parts of the cultural identity of peoples and communities worldwide. Each language showcases its own worldview, and through them, we are able to convey emotions, artistic expressions, and cultural traditions.
